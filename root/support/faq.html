Rewards Program

How does the Heyshorlo Rewards Program work?

The reward system is exclusively available for users who participate in the contest. There are no other options on the website through which users can claim rewards.

How will I receive my reward?

The winner of the contest will be contacted via social media or email within 48 hours. The reward will be provided either as cash or in the form of a coupon, depending on the specific terms of the contest.

Do rewards expire?

If the user does not respond or contact Heyshorlo customer support within 48 hours, the prize will be withdrawn, and the company will reserve the right to that prize.

Can I combine reward with other discounts?

In most cases, yes! You can combine your reward with seasonal sales and promotional discounts. However, reward cannot be combined with certain exclusive offers or limited-time deals. The checkout page will always show you the best possible combination of discounts available for your order.

Will any documents be required to claim the reward?

Yes, basic identification or verification documents may be required to confirm your identity before the reward is issued. The specific requirements will be communicated at the time of contact.

Contests & Giveaways

How often does Heyshorlo run contests?

We run contests and giveaways monthly, with special seasonal contests around major holidays. Our monthly contests typically run from the 1st to the 30th of each month, with winners announced with in 24hours after 30th. You can check our Contest page or follow us on social media for announcements about upcoming contests.

How do I enter Heyshorlo contests?

To participate in the contest, the user must create an engagement video and upload it on social media using the hashtags #heyshorloindia and #heyshorloindiacontest.

What kind of prizes can I win?

The first-place winner will receive a **Premium Card**, the second and third-place winners will receive a **Silver Card**, and participants ranked 4th to 15th will receive a **Brown Card**. Each card comes with a unique gift and exciting offers. If you would like to see the details, please visit the contest page.

what is the rule of contest?

As per the Rule & Regulation in contest page and The user must maintain their position until the end of the month and must still be in that position during the last 48 hours. If they move from their position before the 48 hours, they will not receive the reward, even if they are in the top rank. The company is using this method to prevent fake views and fake followers.

Can I enter multiple times?

Each user is allowed to enter the contest only once. If any user is found engaging in fake activity or using multiple IDs to manipulate views, they will be permanently banned from all Heyshorlo contests.

Products

Are Heyshorlo products cruelty-free?

Absolutely! Heyshorlo is 100% cruelty-free. We never test our products or ingredients on animals, and we don't work with suppliers who conduct animal testing. All our products carry these certifications, which you can verify on our packaging or website.

Are your products vegan?

About 85% of our product line is vegan, and we're working to make this 100%. All vegan products are clearly marked with a green "V" symbol on our website and packaging. Our non-vegan products may contain ingredients like beeswax or carmine, which we're actively seeking plant-based alternatives for.

How can I verify my Heyshorlo product is authentic?

Every Heyshorlo product has a unique verification code under the packaging. You can enter this code on our Verify Product page to confirm authenticity. Authentic products will display full product details and manufacturing date. If you suspect a counterfeit, please contact us immediately with the product details.

What's your return policy?

No, we not offer any kind of return policy

Do you offer samples or testers?

Yes! We offer sample sizes of most products for ₹99 each. You can also request free samples with every order (limit 1 per order and order should be more then 1999). Our physical stores provide free testers for all products without any charges . We encourage trying before buying to ensure you love the product as much as we do!

PARTNER

How can I become a distributor, retailer, or financier?

The distributor , retailer, or financier can apply on our website by registering under the "Apply as Distributor" , "Apply as retailer" ,"Apply as financier" section. Our team will contact them, and after completing the necessary documentation, they can become an authorized Heyshorlo distributor , retailer or financier.

what document will need for distributor?

1. Business Registration 2. Tax Identification Number (TIN) 3. Bank Account Details 4. Identification Proof 5. Address Proof 6. Financial Documents 7. Experience or References 8. Distribution Plan

what document will need for retailer ?

1. Business Registration 2. Tax Identification Number (TIN) 3. Bank Account Details 4. Identification Proof 5. Address Proof 6. Financial Documents 7. Retail License (if required) 8. Store/Website Details 9. Sales Tax Registration (if applicable)

What documents are required to become a financier ?

1. Business Registration 2. Tax Identification Number (TIN) 3. Bank Account Details 4. Identification Proof 5. Address Proof 6. Financial Documents 7. Franchise Agreement 8. Franchise Experience or References 9. Legal Documents (e.g., lease agreement for premises) 10. Proof of Investment Capability 11. Franchisee Application Form
