Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal information under data protection laws (such as GDPR or CCPA). HeySharlo respects your privacy and ensures you can exercise these rights:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data held by us.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a transfer of the data we have collected to another organization, or directly to you.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at heysharloindia@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Payment & Refunds Policy

We appreciate your trust in our platform and are committed to providing a transparent and secure payment process. By making a purchase, you agree to the following terms.

No Refund Policy on Products

All purchases of physical or digital cosmetics products are non-refundable. Once a product is bought, we do not entertain returns, refunds, or exchanges unless the product arrived damaged or defective. Please review all details before making a payment.

Exceptions for Memberships:

Refunds are only considered for card-based services (King Card, Queen Card, VIP Card) if:

  • The card/membership was not activated properly due to our system error.
  • You were incorrectly charged multiple times for the same transaction.

Note: If a membership benefit or discount has been utilized, no refund request will be accepted.

Membership Cancellation Policy

Users can cancel their subscription within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund, provided no benefits have been redeemed. After 24 hours, cancellations will prevent future auto-renewals, but the current billing cycle will not be refunded.
